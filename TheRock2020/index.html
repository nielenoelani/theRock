    Who is "The Rock"?

    Get to know "The Rock" Johnson. Learn his struggles, his success, and his story.

    "The Rock" is successful.

    Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson is one of the most famous and successful actors in the world. He has been in over 40 movies, and many of his movies are blockbusters earning hundreds of millions of dollars. He is a successful film producer as well.

    "The Rock" is disciplined.

    "The Rock" is a disciplined athlete. He gets his rock hard muscles by keeping to strict workout routine. This discipline has led to his becoming one of the most renowned wrestlers in the world.

    "The Rock" is inspiring.

    "The Rock" Johnson is also a motivational speaker. He constantly inspires others with his rags-to-riches story, and how he accomplished the American dream.
